Link Your Bank Account

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up as a seller?

Visit the Seller Portal, complete the registration form with your business details, ID, and selfie, and choose a Basic or Pro plan. Verification takes 1–2 days.

How do I handle buyer disputes?

Respond to buyer issues within 5 days via the Orders section. If unresolved, escalate to support for mediation under the Consumer Protection Act.

Why is my withdrawal delayed?

Standard withdrawals (1–2 days) may be delayed by bank processing or holidays. Immediate withdrawals are faster but incur a R10 fee. Check status in Payments.

How is my data protected?

We use AES-256 encryption and comply with POPIA. Data is securely stored, and breaches are reported within 72 hours with steps to protect your account.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes, upgrade to the Pro plan in Account Settings to access bulk uploads and advanced analytics. Downgrade or cancel anytime.

How do I comply with POPIA?

Obtain buyer consent before sharing data, keep records confidential, and report breaches to support. Non-compliance, as in SAPS (2023), risks penalties.

Product Listing

Create and manage your product listings to attract buyers, ensuring compliance with the Consumer Protection Act (CPA).

Add Products

In the Listings section, create compelling product listings by following these steps. Ensure accuracy to comply with the Consumer Protection Act (CPA).

1
Select Categories

Choose a parent category (e.g., Electronics) and a subcategory (e.g., Smartphones) to help buyers find your product.

2
Basic Information

Enter the product name, brand, and a detailed description. Include key features to attract buyers.

3
Price and Quantity

Set a competitive price and specify available stock. Enable low-stock alerts to avoid overselling.

4
Additional Notes

Add optional notes, such as warranty details, shipping info, or return policies, to inform buyers.

5
Visibility

Choose whether the product is active (visible to buyers) or inactive (hidden from your storefront).

6
Discount

Offer a discount by setting a reduced price or percentage off to boost sales, optional for all listings.

7
Condition

Specify if the product is new, used, or refurbished. Accurate condition details prevent disputes.

8
Product Image

Upload high-quality images (up to 5) showcasing the product from multiple angles. Images are key to buyer trust.

9
Optional Enhancements

Use Pro plan features like promoted listings or bulk uploads to increase visibility and streamline management.
