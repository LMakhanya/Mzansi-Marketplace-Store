Agents are responsible for referring new sellers to the Platform using their unique referral link. Agents earn a signup bonus and ongoing commissions as outlined in the "Fees and Earnings" section of the Support page. Bonuses are paid after a 7-day verification period to prevent abuse. 6. Amendments

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 4 June 2025

Mzansi Marketplace is committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Personal details (e.g., name, email, ID number) provided during registration.
  • Financial information (e.g., bank details) for payout processing.
  • Usage data (e.g., dashboard interactions) via cookies, as permitted by the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act 25 of 2002.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Manage your agent account and process payouts.
  • Track referrals and calculate commissions.
  • Provide support and marketing materials.
  • Comply with legal obligations, such as tax reporting under the Income Tax Act.

By joining the Agent Program, you consent to the processing of your personal information for account management and payouts. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time via your dashboard settings or by contacting support@mzansimarketplace.co.za.

3. Payout Data Handling

Bank details are encrypted using AES-256 encryption and stored securely with our PCI DSS-compliant payment processor, ensuring compliance with POPIA’s security requirements. We do not share financial details with third parties, except as required by law (e.g., South African Revenue Service). In the 2022 Dis-Chem case, inadequate security measures led to a data breach, emphasizing our commitment to robust safeguards.

4. Data Sharing and Cross-Border Transfers

We may share data with trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors) under strict confidentiality agreements. Cross-border data transfers comply with POPIA Section 72, ensuring equivalent protection in recipient countries or through contractual agreements.

5. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Request correction or deletion of data.
  • Withdraw consent for processing (subject to legal obligations).
  • Lodge complaints with the Information Regulator.
Contact support@mzansimarketplace.co.za to exercise these rights.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law (e.g., 5 years for tax records under the Tax Administration Act 28 of 2011). Data is securely deleted thereafter.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. Our cookie policy is available on the Platform, as required by POPIA.

8. Data Breach Notification

In case of a data breach, we will notify you and the Information Regulator within 72 hours, as mandated by POPIA, and take steps to mitigate harm. For example, if a breach occurs, we will notify affected agents via email within 72 hours, provide details of the breach, and offer steps to secure your account, such as updating login credentials.

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact support@mzansimarketplace.co.za. We are committed to resolving issues promptly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid?

All withdrawal requests must be submitted through the Mzansi Marketplace portal and are paid to your saved payout details.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

• Standard minimum withdrawal of R50 incurs no fee and is processed within 1–2 business days.
• Immediate minimum withdrawal of R100 incurs a R10 fee per transaction.
• Withdrawal requests submitted on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

When do I earn commission from a seller?

You earn commission only when the seller is active and subscribed to a paid plan (Basic or Pro). If the seller cancels or becomes inactive, your ongoing commissions will stop. You will still keep any commission earned from their withdrawals while they were on an active paid plan.

How can I increase my earnings?

Refer more sellers, especially those opting for the Pro plan, to maximize signup bonuses. Encourage your sellers to increase their sales, as your ongoing commissions grow with their withdrawals. Aim for higher tiers to unlock better commission rates and benefits.

What support is available?

Basic support is available via email for all agents. Gold and Platinum tier agents receive priority support, and Platinum agents get a dedicated account manager. Contact us below for assistance.

Are my earnings secure?

Your earnings are securely processed through our PCI DSS-compliant payment system. Payments are made to your verified bank account, and we conduct regular audits to ensure accuracy and security.

How is my data protected?

We comply with POPIA, using AES-256 encryption and secure servers to protect your data. You can request access or deletion of your information at any time via support@mzansimarketplace.co.za.
